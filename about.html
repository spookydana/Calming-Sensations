The Coding Sisters

(Hamreet Kaur, Dana Pomeroy, Aries Graves)

Who Are We?

We are a trio of high school girls participating in the Girls Who Code summer immersion program. In this program, we spent seven weeks at Amazon (in Seattle, Washington),
learning more about computer science, while getting introduced to different tech jobs. The final two weeks of this program we were assigned into groups based on our interests,
with our interest in website design, we were assigned to a destressor app. Quickly changing the name to Calming Sensation, and to a website, we had a great start. We created a
prototype in google docs, and assigned each sister different tasks. We had one week to create a functioning website, and it was crazy. But, we knew our website would be great
no matter what.

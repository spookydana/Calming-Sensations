Nature Music

Rainforest Sounds

The sound of the rainforest, birds chirping, wind blowing, and flowing water are some of the few things that help
reduce stress and induce a calming state. Also, the sound of nature helps your nervous system recover faster after a stressful event,
showing you can truly benefit from the results of listening to nature sounds.

Campfire Sounds

The sound of a crackling campfire helps people calm down, but the longer you watch and listen, the more relax you become.
While having some calm attention to this campfire, you’re also helping reduce your anxiety and your blood pressure.
These sounds helps you focus and brightens your mood, and you don’t even have to do anything while listening to it!

Ocean Sounds

The sound of crashing waves is one of the most known white noise, as it helps people calm down tremendously.
It helps improve stress responses and overall health. Use these music tools, and maybe the help of our exercise pages
to create a calm, peaceful environment to relax.
